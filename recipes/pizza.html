Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish that consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with various ingredients such as cheese, tomato sauce, meats, vegetables, and herbs. It is baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

This pizza recipe creates a crispy crust and a flavorful sauce, topped with fresh ingredients and melted cheese for the ultimate pizza experience. Perfect for a family meal or a pizza party with friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni or other toppings
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (bell peppers, mushrooms, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and grease a pizza pan with olive oil.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and transfer it to the pizza pan.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese, Parmesan, and your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza in the oven for 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with oregano, and serve hot.
