Burger

Description

A burger is a classic American fast food consisting of a beef patty, placed between two buns, often accompanied by lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, pickles, and various condiments. It's an easy-to-make yet satisfying meal that can be customized to suit any taste.

This recipe features a juicy beef patty, homemade sauce, and all the classic toppings that make the perfect burger. Whether you prefer a simple burger or one packed with extras, this recipe has you covered.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1/2 cup cheese slices (cheddar or American)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Tomato slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop pan over medium heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 equal patties, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties on the grill or pan for 4-5 minutes per side, until fully cooked.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns lightly on the grill or in a pan.
  5. Assemble the burgers: place the patty on the bottom bun, top with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  6. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top bun, then close the burger and serve.
