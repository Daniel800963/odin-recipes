Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty, comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

This recipe combines traditional flavors with an easy-to-follow method to ensure perfect results every time. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a beginner, this lasagna will impress everyone at your table.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook the ground beef until browned. Add marinara sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish: start with a layer of sauce, then noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, then let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
